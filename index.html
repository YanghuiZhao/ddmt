Research

Research.

Pillar 01

Submarine structure & lithospheric evolution.

We image basin architecture, crustal structure, and tectonic-magmatic coupling with seismic, gravity, and bathymetric data across divergent plate settings.

  • Passive margins of the China seas
  • Western Pacific back-arc basins
  • Mid-ocean ridge initiation, spreading, and post-spreading deformation

Pillar 02

ML for seamounts & intraplate volcanism.

We use Gaussian processes, random forests, and unsupervised clustering to recover seamount morphometry, elastic thickness patterns, and tectonic controls on intraplate volcanism.

  • Pacific seamount provinces
  • Elastic thickness and lithospheric strength
  • Volcanism in oceanic and back-arc basins
Seismic Bathymetry Gravity Tectonic reconstruction Gaussian processes Random forests Unsupervised clustering
